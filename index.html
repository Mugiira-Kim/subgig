What Is Sub Gig All About?

A subcontractor is an individual or (in many cases) a business that signs a contract to perform part or all of the obligations of another's contract.
Put simply the role of a subcontractor is to execute the job they are hired by the contractor for.
At Sub Gig, we identify gig opportunities in your location , help you enroll, and we do all the work as you take care of other business. .
